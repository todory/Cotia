ABOUT KOREA

korea is located in the Korea peninsula. 'East Sea', 'west Sea', and 'Southern Sea' is surrounding our country.
We can also call our country ‘Han-guk', 'South Korea', and 'Republic of Korea'. Our language is Korean which is regarded as the most scientific word in the world.

Hanbok is a unique Korean clothing that has been handed down since ancient times. The straight line and some curves are harmonized to create a beautiful look, and women's clothes are especially neat and small, with short jackets and generous skirts....

Korea is a country rich in tourism resources.
Traditional and modern cultures coexist, allowing tourists to experience various experiences, and each region has a clear identity, allowing them to enjoy various cultures, natural environments, and food.
